Girls in Tech – क्यों ज़रूरी है Representation

📌 Why This Blog Matters

“Main ek middle-class background se hoon, aur jab maine coding start ki, mere aas paas koi ladki nahi thi jo tech field mein actively kaam kar rahi thi.”

Society ka common belief tha: "Tech is not for girls."

🚫 Challenges Faced by Girls in Tech

  • ⚠ Confidence ki kami
  • ⚠ Support system ka na hona
  • ⚠ Judgement from others – "Coding? Are you sure?"
  • ⚠ Role models ki kami

🌟 Why Representation Matters

Jab ek ladki dusri ladki ko coding karte hue dekhti hai, to wo sochti hai: "Main bhi kar sakti hoon." Yeh ek powerful permission jaisi hoti hai.

Girls bring:

  • 🎨 Creativity
  • 🤝 Empathy
  • 🧠 Different Thinking

Jab sab logon ka input hota hai, tabhi balanced innovation hoti hai.

🙋‍♀ Be a Role Model

Tum jaise ladkiyon ka blog banana, portfolio banana – ye dusri girls ko inspire karta hai.
Tumhari journey kisi aur ke liye shuruaat ban sakti hai.

“Role model banne ke liye perfect hona zaruri nahi hota – bas real hona zaruri hota hai.”

📝 Conclusion

"Coding sirf skill nahi hai, ek voice hai — aur har ladki ko apni voice dikhani chahiye."
