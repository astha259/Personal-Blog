Rejected but Rising – My First Internship Attempt

📍 How It All Started

Maine apni pehli web development internship ke liye apply kiya tha, full excitement ke sath. I had hopes that I might get selected. Lekin jab result aaya — "Sorry, not selected." Yeh dekh kar thoda sad feel hua.

😔 The Struggles I Faced

Mujhe JavaScript ki samajh kam thi, and English communication bhi weak thi. Sometimes mujhe samajh hi nahi aata tha ki instructions kya keh rahe hain. Is wajah se self-doubt bhi aaya: "Kya main is field ke layak hoon?"

💡 What This Rejection Taught Me

  • ✔ Har rejection ek signal hota hai ki abhi aur seekhna baaki hai
  • ✔ Skills ko strong banana zaruri hai, especially JavaScript & basic projects
  • ✔ English perfect hona zaruri nahi — samajhna aur express karna kaafi hai
  • ✔ Daily practice se confidence aata hai, chahe time kam ho

🔥 Turning Rejection into Motivation

Pehle to dukh hua, lekin phir socha — "Is baar nahi to agle baar sahi!"
Maine decide kiya ki har din kuchh na kuchh naya seekhna hai — chahe ek choti si concept ho ya ek simple project. Dheere-dheere mujhe coding ka logic samajh aane laga, aur English me likhna bhi comfortable ho gaya. Iss process ke saath mera confidence bhi grow karta gaya.

💬 Final Thoughts

Internship ka selection nahi hua, but it gave me a clear direction. Ab main apne aap pe kaam kar rahi hoon — aur har din better ban rahi hoon.
“Rejection mera end nahi tha, balki meri growth ka start tha.”
